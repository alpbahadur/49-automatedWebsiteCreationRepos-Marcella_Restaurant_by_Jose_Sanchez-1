A Story Rooted in Love

Marcella Restaurant is more than just a culinary destination—it's a tribute to the woman who inspired Chef José Sánchez's passion for cooking: his grandmother, Marcella.

Named after her favorite flower, the violet orchid, our restaurant embodies beauty, elegance, and the warmth of family traditions. Every dish tells a story, blending authentic Puerto Rican roots with international culinary techniques.

Since 2009, Chef Sánchez has crafted his culinary journey across kitchens in and beyond Puerto Rico. At Marcella, he brings his vision to life—fresh, locally-sourced ingredients transformed into creative Creole gastronomy that honors tradition while embracing innovation.
